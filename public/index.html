About Me

Hello, world! My name is Omar, and I am a software developer. I have so many ideas for great applications, and I want for them to become real so people can use them and benefit from them. Previously I worked in a hospital because I hold a degree in medical sciences. However, my job was not that fun. I felt I could do better if I did the job I love. I always loved to learn programming and coding, so I quit my job for that very purpose. Now I am a full-stack web developer and I am so excited to be so. In the past, I came up with a software idea that can solve many problems in hospitals and the health system in my country. Now I started working on it, hoping someday it will help people everywhere. So far, I have built many applications, worked with teams, and led teams in different projects. I always make sure to get software working and functioning properly and on time. Having worked on many projects, I massively improved my leadership and management skills. One of my best practices is to look to life problems as potential sources of novel and innovative tech solutions, so I analyze these problems thoroughly and try to come up with creative app ideas that can have an impact on society.
